About the Job

The Production Foreman plays a crucial role in overseeing manufacturing operations at Moha Soft Drinks Industry Plc. This full-time position, based in an office setting in Addis Ababa, requires strong leadership and coordination skills to manage production teams effectively. The foreman ensures that production goals are met while maintaining high-quality standards and operational efficiency. This role also involves coaching team members and fostering a collaborative work environment.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Supervise daily production activities to ensure adherence to safety and quality standards.
  • Coordinate and schedule production tasks to optimize workflow and meet deadlines.
  • Monitor production metrics and implement improvements to enhance efficiency.
  • Conduct regular training sessions to develop team skills and knowledge of manufacturing processes.
  • Oversee the use of ERP tools (SAP) and digital production platforms to streamline operations.
  • Maintain accurate records of production output and equipment performance.
  • Collaborate with other departments to ensure smooth operations and resolve any issues.
  • Implement best practices in manufacturing operations to reduce waste and improve quality.
  • Facilitate effective communication within the team and with upper management.
  • Evaluate team performance and provide constructive feedback for continuous improvement.

About You

Required Skills:

  • Build strong working relationships with team members and other stakeholders.
  • Coordinate multiple tasks effectively while working under pressure.
  • Deliver quality work consistently within established deadlines.
  • Coach and mentor team members to enhance their performance and development.
  • Possess good knowledge of manufacturing systems and processes.
  • Utilize ERP tools (SAP) and digital production platforms proficiently.
  • Demonstrate strong teamwork abilities to foster a collaborative environment.
  • Analyze production data to identify trends and areas for improvement.
  • Adapt to changing priorities and maintain flexibility in a dynamic work environment.
  • Resolve conflicts and challenges through effective problem-solving skills.

Desired Skills:

  • Exhibit a keen attention to detail in all aspects of production management.
  • Have experience in supervisory roles within a manufacturing environment.
  • Demonstrate strong organizational skills to manage multiple priorities efficiently.
  • Exhibit time-management skills to ensure timely completion of tasks.
  • Maintain a proactive approach to identifying and addressing potential production issues.

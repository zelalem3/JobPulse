About the Job

Vacancy Announcement

Key Responsibilities

  • Prepare and monitor project budgets.
  • Track project expenditures and ensure budget compliance.
  • Prepare project cost reports and financial analyses.
  • Process project invoices and payment certificates.
  • Monitor project profitability and cash flow.
  • Coordinate with Project Managers on project financial performance.
  • Ensure project expenses comply with contract terms and company policies.
  • Prepare financial reports for management and project stakeholders.
  • Maintain accurate financial records for each project.

Company: BESYS Technologies PLC


Employment Type: Full-Time


Duty Station: Addis Ababa (ICT Park near to Goro)

About You

Qualification

  • BA Degree in Accounting, Accounting & Finance, or a related field.

Experience

  • Minimum 4 years of relevant experience in accounting, with at least 2 years in project accounting, construction accounting, or a similar role.
  • Experience in construction, engineering, or ICT project environments is highly desirable.
  • Experience using ERP systems is an advantage.

Required Competencies

  • Strong understanding of project costing and budgeting.
  • Excellent analytical and reporting skills.
  • Ability to work with multidisciplinary project teams.
  • Good communication and organizational skills
